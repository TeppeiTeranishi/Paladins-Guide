Play Like a Pro

Ready to take your Paladins game to the next level? We’ve got you covered! This series of brief tutorials will give you all the tips and tricks you’ll need to become a more experienced Paladins player and achieve victory in the Realm!

Customize Your Loadout

Creating Loadouts for your favorite Champions opens up all-new strategies for how you play Paladins. Check out our Loadout Tutorial to learn how to start creating your own custom loadouts for all of your favorite Champions!

Use Your Talents

Every Champion in Paladins has 3 unique Talents available. Combine your talents with Custom Loadouts to create the most effective Champions possible Check out our Talent Tutorial for more information!

Learn About VGS

In Paladins, communication is key. Learn more about how to utilize the VGS system to communicate more effectively with your teammates in our Intro to VGS tutorial.

Advanced Item Strategies

Once you’ve mastered the basics of Items, it’s time to move on to a more strategic approach! Our Item Strategy Tutorial contains more advanced information about how to utilize the item shop during each match to maximize your effectiveness on the battlefield.

Welcome to Ranked

Ready for the ultimate challenge? Unlocked Ranked mode at account level 15 and start testing your skill against the best players in Paladins. Check out our Ranked Tutorial for more information on entering the world of Ranked Paladins play!
