Master the Basics

Ready to learn the basics of Paladins? Here’s a brief rundown of all the information you’ll need to know to start playing - and winning!

Item Shop Basics

Purchase Defense, Utility, Healing, and Offense items during Paladins matches to increase your effectiveness on the battlefield. Knowing what each item does and when to purchase them is a pivotal step in developing your skill in Paladins. Check out the Item Shop Basics video for more information on the Item Shop.

Game Modes

Paladins offers 3 different game modes. Whether you prefer the objective based gameplay of Siege and Onslaught, or just want to outplay your enemies in traditional Team Deathmatch, learn more below about how to achieve victory in Paladins.

Link Your Accounts

Play your Paladins account across multiple platforms and earn exciting rewards by linking your accounts. Check out our Account Linking video for information on how to quickly jump into the world of cross-progression and free rewards in Paladins!
